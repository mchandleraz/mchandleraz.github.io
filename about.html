About

As a Front-End Developer, I specialize in developing mobile-first responsive web designs with semantic markup and styling using HTML5/CSS3, and progressive enhancement with JavaScript (via jQuery where appropriate). I've worked extensively with WordPress as a blogging platform as well as a CMS.

I've worked on projects from e-mail newsletters to database-driven CMS-powered websites for businesses. I'm the founder and creator of Gumpshun, an HTML5 webapp for vintage motorcycle enthusiasts.

I use modern web development tools like Yeoman, Bootstrap, Ember, and Grunt Gulp.js to build quickly and efficiently. This means your project ships on time, works and looks great across devices, and you save money.
